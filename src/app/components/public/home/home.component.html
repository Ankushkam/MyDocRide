Our Mission

At MyDocRide we know you want to provide the highest level of care for your patients. In order to do that you need patients to show up to their appointments. Currently many seniors and disabled people struggle to get to their necessary appointments, which makes you feel like you could do more. No one should have to deal with transportation as a barrier to their medical needs.

We understand how you feel and we are here to help, that's why we created MyDocRide. The medical provider schedules the ride, the patient is taken to their appointment, and the driver is paid.
